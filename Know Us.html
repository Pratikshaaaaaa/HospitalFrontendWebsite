About Ashtavinayak Hospital

Ashtvinayak Hospital founded in the year 2006 by Shri. Suresh Chandra Agarwal to provide quality health service to the people living in Navi Mumbai and adjoining area, but soon it established as the most value-added, cost-effective health care center in the entire region.

The hospital is situated in separate premise spread over 16000 sq. ft. It is centrally located with adequate parking area. As situated in the heart of Panvel city the hospital is connected with a good public transport network.

With a significant infrastructural resource that it has raised over the years, both in terms of Advance diagnostic equipment, state of the art surgery facilities and a team of acclaimed Doctors and caring nurses under the Dynamic direction of Dr. Akshdeep Agarwal. Our hospital now provides the cure and treatment for an array of ailments.

Ashtvinayak Hospital has been appreciated and cherished by Media and section of the Society for the prompt and professional service is provided to the injured victims. Similarly, our hospital delivers quality and specialized health care in Cardiology, Urology, Orthopaedics, Laparoscopic surgery, Minimally invasive surgery, and many others. Thanks to the highly acclaimed team of Physicians and Surgeons who performed even the most complex surgery with ease. Our support staffs are specially trained in patient-centered care.
