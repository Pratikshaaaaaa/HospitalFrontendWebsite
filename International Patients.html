International Patient Care at Ashtavinayak Hospital

Ashtvinayak Hospital offers seamless experience to patients visiting India for medical reasons. We have a dedicated team to cater to International patients.

International patients can use our services of multi-lingual support and all the coordination can happen in your preferred language.

The medical attention you receive is absolutely world class and we ensure the patient is given the best care possible.

We are committed to providing the safest and highest quality healthcare services to all our patients. We work together to ensure that you receive personalized and state-of-the-art care during and post your treatment here.

We are a patient-friendly hospital and aim at providing quality healthcare services to international patients who are looking out for medical tourism in India. Our medical services and treatment options, and the facilities and amenities that we provide to our patients are pocket-friendly and are at par with the international standards.
